Top 10

These are the games I’m playing the most right now. I’ll keep updating this list as I discover new favorites.

1A

Roblox

An endless universe of player-made games — I love hopping between modes with friends, unlocking items, and discovering new worlds.

1B

Roblox Forge

Forge is a game on the Roblox platform.

My favorite Roblox experience right now — fast, creative, and super fun to grind with friends.

2

Pokémon Legends: Z-A

A new Pokémon adventure I’m hyped for — exploring, battling, and collecting in a fresh open-style world.

3

Minecraft

The ultimate creativity game — building huge projects, surviving with friends, and experimenting with crazy redstone ideas.

4

Clash Royale

Quick strategy battles that never get old — timing, decks, and mind games in 3-minute matches.

5

Mario Kart World

Chaotic racing with perfect “one more race” energy — drifting, shortcuts, and hilarious comebacks.

6

Super Smash Bros

The best party fighter — mastering characters, pulling off combos, and laughing at surprise KOs.

7

Fortnite

Fast, competitive, and always changing — I like the variety of modes, events, and clutch endgames.

8

Star Wars: Jedi Survivor

A cinematic Star Wars adventure with awesome lightsaber combat, exploration, and big boss fights.

9

Hogwarts Legacy

Open-world magic school vibes — exploring Hogwarts, learning spells, and going on quests in the wizarding world.

10

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

One of the most creative adventure games ever — building crazy inventions and exploring everywhere.
