This is what I’m playing right now (and what I’m excited about). I’ll rotate this list as new stuff drops.

Roblox
Roblox is a huge universe of games and experiences. I like trying new worlds, building, and hanging out with friends in-game.
Forge (Roblox)
Forge is my favorite Roblox experience right now. It’s fast, competitive, and feels like you’re leveling up every session.
Clash Royale
A real-time strategy game where deck choices and timing matter. Quick matches, big comebacks, and lots of ways to improve.
Minecraft
The best sandbox for creativity: building, survival, and experimenting with redstone. I love starting big projects and making them better over time.
Fortnite
Battle Royale + tons of modes and events. It’s the most fun when you’re in a squad and everyone is trying different strategies.
Pokémon Legends: Z-A
I’m hyped for this next Pokémon adventure. The world, the battles, and exploring new areas are what I’m most excited about.
Hogwarts Legacy
An open-world wizard adventure with spells, quests, and exploring Hogwarts. I love the atmosphere and discovering hidden details.
Super Smash Bros.
The ultimate couch-multiplayer battle game. Great for friendly rivalries, practice sessions, and chaotic matches with friends.
Mario Kart World
Racing madness with perfect drifts, shortcuts, and rematches. The best kind of competitive fun with friends and family.
